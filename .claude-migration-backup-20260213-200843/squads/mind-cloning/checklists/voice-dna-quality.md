## Extraction Completeness

- [ ] **10+ power words** with context and weight
- [ ] **5+ signature phrases** with usage triggers
- [ ] **3+ favorite metaphors** with concept mapping
- [ ] **3+ recurring stories** with lesson and trigger
- [ ] **Story structure mapped** (opening, build-up, payoff, callback)
- [ ] **Voice dimensions filled** (all 7 dimensions rated 1-10)
- [ ] **3+ communication anti-patterns** (never_say / never_do)
- [ ] **2+ automatic rejections** (immune system triggers)
- [ ] **1+ voice paradox documented** (with "DO NOT RESOLVE" instruction)
- [ ] **Identity statement written** ("{Name} writes/speaks like ___")

## Quality Signals

- [ ] Power words are **triangulated** (appear in 3+ sources)
- [ ] Signature phrases include **exact quotes** from sources
- [ ] Metaphors are **unique to the expert** (not generic)
- [ ] Stories include **specific details** (names, dates, places)
- [ ] Tone dimensions are **consistent with sources** (not guessed)
- [ ] Anti-patterns have **rationale** (why they avoid it)
- [ ] Paradoxes are **genuine contradictions** (not misunderstandings)

## Scoring

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 10/10 | **EXCELLENT** | Proceed with confidence |
| 8-9/10 | **PASS** | Proceed, minor gaps acceptable |
| 6-7/10 | **NEEDS WORK** | Re-extract weak phases |
| <6/10 | **FAIL** | Significant rework needed |
