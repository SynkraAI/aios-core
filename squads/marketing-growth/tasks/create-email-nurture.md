# Create Email Nurture Sequence

**Task:** `create-email-nurture`
**Agent:** @lead-copywriter (via Copy Squad)
**Type:** Creation

---

## Description

Criar sequencia de emails de nutricao (5-10 emails) para leads frios.

---

## Input

```yaml
objetivo: 'nutrir | educar | vender-soft'
avatar: 'Descricao do publico'
num_emails: 7
frequencia: '2-3 dias entre emails'
lead_magnet: 'Nome do lead magnet que gerou o lead'
```

---

## Process

### 1. Estrategia (@lead-copywriter)

- Define jornada da sequencia
- Seleciona framework (Ryan Deiss methodology)

### 2. Copy (Copy Squad)

- Email 1: Welcome + entrega lead magnet
- Email 2: Quick win (dica pratica)
- Email 3: Story (conexao emocional)
- Email 4: Value (conteudo profundo)
- Email 5: Case study (social proof)
- Email 6: Soft sell (primeira oferta)
- Email 7: Direct sell (conversao)

### 3. Quality Gate

- Checklist: content-quality + legal-lgpd

---

## Output

```
Por email:
- Subject line (max 50 chars)
- Preview text
- Body (HTML ou texto)
- CTA
- Dia de envio (D0, D2, D4...)
- Segmento (all | engaged | clicked)
```
